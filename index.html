Our Story

A Family Legacy Since 1995

What started as a simple dream to bring the tastes of home to the Filipino community in Southern California has grown into a beloved neighborhood restaurant.

"Every dish we serve carries the spirit of our family kitchen back in the Philippines — cooked with the same love, the same recipes, the same heart."

For nearly three decades, Maria's Kusina has been a gathering place for families, a taste of home for homesick Filipinos, and a gateway to authentic Filipino cuisine for adventurous food lovers across LA and Orange County.

From our signature Crispy Pata to our beloved Lumpia Shanghai, every dish is prepared fresh daily using time-honored recipes and the finest ingredients.
